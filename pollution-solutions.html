What can you do?
  • Reduce your pollution exposure
    • Try to stay inside or wear a pollution mask when air quality is poor
    • Close your windows during peak pollution times

  • Reduce your pollution output
    • Take public transit
    • Ride fuel efficient cars

  • Civic Engagement
    • Share air quality statistics on social media
    • Send complaints when you see illegal polluting activities like trash burning
